Australia will be a nation of abundance with limitless access to energy, food, water, labour, intelligence, and transport. To accelerate that future, we want to support young Australian talent with unbounded dreams who will make this future possible.

A&Mu Fund disburses one $100 micro-grant every month. To be eligible, you must:

  • be located in Australia,
  • be 21 years old or younger, and
  • describe how your talents and skills (of any discipline) could contribute to pulling an abundant future closer or expands what becomes possible in that future

Though our micro-grants are small, we hope that you see this as one representation of our belief in you.

If an abundant future in Australia excites you, apply!

A&Mu Fund serves Age of Abundance and is funded by M. Z. Mitchell Zheng.
